For returning NRIs, the RNOR status is a goldmine. It allows your foreign income to remain tax-exempt in India for up to 3 years after your return.

Non-Resident status is strictly math-based. Stay in India for less than 182 days to maintain your tax-efficient NR status. We track this for you.
